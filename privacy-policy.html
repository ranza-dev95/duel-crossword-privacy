Privacy Policy

Duel Crossword by Ranza Dev

Effective date: May 26, 2026

Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Duel Crossword collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the mobile application. By using Duel Crossword, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

Duel Crossword may collect and process the following information:

  • Account information, such as your Firebase user ID, display name, email address when available, sign-in provider, and profile photo.
  • Profile information you choose to provide, including your player name and custom avatar image.
  • Game information, including scores, match history, leaderboard entries, coins, saved games, friend requests, duel invites, room status, and multiplayer presence.
  • Purchase and subscription information needed to activate Pro features, such as purchase status and product identifiers from Google Play or Apple App Store.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad interaction data processed by Google Mobile Ads when ads are shown to free users.
  • Technical information, such as device platform, app activity, diagnostics, server logs, authentication tokens, and security signals used to operate and protect the service.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Create and manage user accounts and player profiles.
  • Run solo games, online duels, race matches, leaderboards, coins, friend features, saved games, and match history.
  • Provide Pro subscription benefits, including removing ads and enabling Pro gameplay benefits.
  • Show ads to free users and rewarded ads when users choose to earn coins.
  • Detect abuse, prevent fraud, maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and improve app reliability.
  • Comply with legal, platform, and store requirements.

Ads and Pro Subscription

Duel Crossword uses Google Mobile Ads for banner ads and rewarded ads. Free users may see ads. Pro users are intended to receive an ad-free experience while their subscription is active.

Purchases and subscriptions are processed by the app store provider, such as Google Play Billing or Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your full payment card details.

Third-Party Services

Duel Crossword uses third-party services that may process data according to their own privacy policies, including:

  • Firebase Authentication, Firebase Realtime Database, Firebase Storage, Firebase Cloud Functions, and related Firebase services.
  • Google Sign-In, when you choose to sign in with Google.
  • Google Mobile Ads and Google AdMob.
  • Google Play Billing and Apple App Store in-app purchase services.
  • Cloud database and server infrastructure used to generate puzzles and run multiplayer features.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate Duel Crossword, process authentication, serve ads, process purchases, host data, provide multiplayer features, prevent fraud, or comply with legal obligations.

Some profile and gameplay information, such as display name, avatar, leaderboard score, online presence, match result, or friend status, may be visible to other users as part of normal app functionality.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as needed to provide the app, maintain user accounts, support gameplay history, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Some server logs may be retained for a limited period for security and diagnostics.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, including Firebase authentication, database rules, secure transport, and access controls. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.
