Delete Account Request

Duel Crossword by Ranza Dev

Effective date: May 26, 2026

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request deletion of your Duel Crossword account and associated app data by emailing Ranza Dev. Please send the request from the email address connected to your Duel Crossword account when possible.

Email Deletion Request

If the button does not open your email app, send your request manually to ranzadev95@gmail.com with the subject: Duel Crossword Account Deletion Request.

Information to Include

To help us locate and verify your account, include:

  • Your Duel Crossword account email address or sign-in email.
  • Your player name in Duel Crossword.
  • Your Firebase user ID, if you know it.
  • Any additional information that can help verify account ownership.

What Will Be Deleted

After verifying your request, we will delete or anonymize app account data associated with your Duel Crossword account, including where applicable:

  • Account profile data, such as player name, profile photo, avatar, and account identifiers stored by Duel Crossword.
  • Coin wallet data, saved games, match queues, duel invites, friend requests, friendships, and presence records.
  • Solo and multiplayer gameplay records associated with your account where deletion is technically and operationally feasible.
  • Custom profile avatar files stored for the app.

Data That May Be Retained

Some information may be retained when necessary, including:

  • Records required for security, fraud prevention, abuse investigation, legal compliance, accounting, or dispute resolution.
  • Store purchase records maintained by Google Play or Apple App Store. These are controlled by the relevant store provider.
  • Server logs and backups for a limited period before they are overwritten or deleted through normal retention cycles.
  • Aggregated or anonymized gameplay statistics that no longer identify you.

Processing Time

We aim to respond to deletion requests within 30 days. Some backup or log data may take longer to disappear from all systems, but it will not be used for normal app features after account deletion is completed.

Subscriptions

Deleting your Duel Crossword account does not automatically cancel an active subscription through Google Play or Apple App Store. Please cancel subscriptions from your store account settings to stop future billing.

Privacy Policy

For more information about how Duel Crossword handles data, read the Privacy Policy.
